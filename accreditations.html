Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital and Research Center is accredited by
National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH) since 24th September 2019
National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) since 19th March 2007

 

 

National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH)

NABH Certificate Number : H-2019-0663

Quality Policy:
Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital believes in highest professional competence with a human touch and will deliver excellent medical treatment to everyone. We will follow rational and ethical medical practices implicitly. We are committed to medico-social services.

Scope of accreditation:
Please find the: NABH accreditation certificate

 

 

 

National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL)

NABL Certificate Number : MC-2177

Quality Policy:
The laboratory will strive to provide diagnostic services of best quality to its patients through a competent, motivated team of laboratory staff at a reasonable cost, for an accurate and timely diagnosis on an ethical basis and will be committed to achieve continual quality improvement.

Scope of accreditation:
Please find the: NABL accreditation certificate

 

 

 

National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH)

NABH Certificate Number : EC-CT-2018-0012

Quality Policy:
We strive to promote scientifically sound and ethical health research in the best interest of patient care.

Scope of accreditation:
Please find the: NABH accreditation certificate
